Why isn't the bot working?                                                                 

                                    

The Guilded bot for Discord is no longer supported.  Notifications are now handled using webhooks.

Once you create a webhook bot in your Discord Server Settings menu, copy its Webhook URL and paste it into the Webhook field of your Guilded channel, located in its Settings menu.

This will enable notifications to be delivered to your server. Webhooks are supported for all Guilded channel types.

For more information on creating webhooks, check out our Outgoing and Incoming Webhook articles.
